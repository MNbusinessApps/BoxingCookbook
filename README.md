## About This Cookbook

This cookbook contains 400+ high-protein recipes specifically designed for amateur boxers, MMA fighters, and weight-class athletes who must make weight while maintaining peak performance. Every recipe has been engineered with precision macronutrient targeting to support aggressive fat loss without sacrificing muscle mass.

### Key Features

- **400+ recipes** optimized for fat loss while preserving muscle
- **Precise macronutrient information** for every recipe
- **Organized by meal type** and protein source
- **Chef-inspired plating instructions** for professional presentation
- **GitHub Pages ready** for easy deployment

## The Boxer's Philosophy

> "Every meal is an opportunity to fuel your championship dreams. Eat with purpose, eat with flavor, eat to win."

### Core Principles

1. **High Protein Intake** - 30-50g per serving to maximize muscle retention
2. **Low Carb Approach** - Fibrous vegetables only, no starchy carbs or sugars
3. **Healthy Fats** - Essential for hormone production and joint health
4. **Bold Flavors** - No compromise on taste for nutrition
